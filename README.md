# Sales-Performance-Analysis-Dashboard
Interactive sales dashboard built with Excel, Pivot Tables, and Slicers to analyze retail performance across different regions and age groups.


<h1> Project Overview</h1>
<p>
  This project features an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze retail sales performance.
  The goal was to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights regarding revenue drivers, customer demographics, and regional success.
</p>

<h2>Key Insights from the Data</h2>
<p>
Top Performer: Bikes are the primary revenue driver, contributing significantly more than Accessories or Clothing.

Geographic Stronghold: The United States is the leading market in terms of total profit, followed by Australia.

Customer Profile: The Adult (35-64) age group represents the most profitable demographic, suggesting that marketing efforts should remain focused on this segment.

Gender Distribution: Sales are nearly balanced between Male and Female customers, indicating a broad market appeal across genders.
</p>

<h2>Technical Features</h2>
<p>
Data Structuring: Organized raw data into structured tables for better scalability.

Pivot Tables: Used for complex data aggregation across multiple dimensions (Age, Gender, Region, and Category).

Data Visualization: Donut and Pie Charts for categorical revenue and gender splits.

Column Charts for regional profit comparison.

Bar Charts to track performance across age groups.

Line Charts to represent the variation of revenue across the years starting from 2011 to 2016

Interactivity: Integrated Slicers (Age-Group) to allow users to filter the entire dashboard dynamically.
</p>
